Purchase Protection

What is Purchase Protection?

At Bonds Mall, your satisfaction and peace of mind are our top priorities. Our Purchase Protection program is designed to safeguard your purchases against unforeseen issues, ensuring that your shopping experience remains worry-free.

Coverage Details

  • Damage Protection: If your purchased item arrives damaged or is damaged within 30 days, we will repair, replace, or refund it.
  • Theft Protection: If your item is stolen within 60 days of purchase, we offer a replacement or refund upon verification.
  • Defect Protection: Coverage for manufacturing defects found within 90 days of purchase.
  • Return & Refund Assistance: We assist you in hassle-free returns and refunds for eligible products.

90-Day Purchase Protection Policies

In-Store Purchases

For items purchased at any Bonds Mall physical retail location, the 90-day Purchase Protection covers:

  • Manufacturing defects or faults occurring within 90 days from the date of purchase.
  • Damage due to shipping or handling issues (if applicable).
  • Item replacement or full refund if a valid claim is made within the 90-day period.

To claim, please retain your physical receipt and visit the store where you made your purchase or contact our support team.

Online Purchases

For purchases made through Bonds Mall’s official website or mobile app, the 90-day Purchase Protection covers:

  • Manufacturing defects or faults occurring within 90 days from the delivery date.
  • Damage occurring during shipping covered under our shipping insurance.
  • Easy replacement or refund process through our online customer portal or support team.

To claim, keep your digital receipt and order confirmation email. Contact support via support@bondsmall.com or phone.

How to Claim Purchase Protection

  1. Contact our customer support team within the protection period via support@bondsmall.com or call 1-800-551-1482.
  2. Provide your purchase receipt and details about the issue.
  3. Follow the instructions provided by our support team for repair, replacement, or refund processing.
Please keep your original purchase receipt and packaging until the protection period expires.

Terms and Conditions

Purchase Protection applies only to items bought directly from Bonds Mall. Items damaged due to misuse, normal wear and tear, or unauthorized repairs are not covered. Please refer to our full Terms and Conditions for more information.
